Target Audience

Our target audiance are people in florid that want to go outside and enjoy good weather. They will be checking the site for up to date local weather in hopes to expolore and have a good time out doors.

Personas

Persona #1

Name: Jerry McCall
Age: 65
Profession: Retired
General: Jerry lives with his wife Susan. Every morning Jerry enjoys a brisk walk around the neighborhood. Part of his morning routine is checking the weather to see what he should wear. This is usually done on his iPad that he uses to read most nights.

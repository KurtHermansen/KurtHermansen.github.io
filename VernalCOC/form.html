Member Levels

Each level gets the privileges mentioned in the above tier.

Non-Profit ($100): Basic access to events and advertising opportunity.

Bronze ($200): Event advertising, directory listing, and early access to events.

Silver ($400): Home page advertising, full print ads and mention at events, reserved booth for local expo shows.

Gold ($1,000): Business trainings via monthly entrepreneurial lunch and learn, Social media posts, radio advertising with events advertising.

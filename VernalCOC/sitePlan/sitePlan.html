Site Purpose

This site is to provide the community and local business owner with information about local business. This will include local event and promotions. Local Business owners can plan events together and get their business out there for the community to see.

Target Audience

Our target audience will be local business owners. They will be looking to have more reach and influence in the community. They will come to the site to help collaborate with other local businesses and get other related local business news.
